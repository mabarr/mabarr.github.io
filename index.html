What You Can Do In Athens

Athens has all sorts of places to explore. One of the most popular is the Acropolis, an ancient city of ruins that contains Parthenon. The Parthenon is almost 2,500 years old and you get to walk around it. Rad. Visiting Beaches in Athens

According to visitgreece.gr, the best beach to visit in Athens is Cape Souonio! The beach is in a prime location because of its proximity to various ancient ruins. Swimming is a must at any of the beaches in Athens, and if you stick around till sunset you can see the Temple of Posiedon from the shore. According to TripSavvy,"While there, you'll be at one point of a magical triangle which the ancient Greeks enjoyed - from Sounion, you could see the Temple of Aphaia on the island of Aegina, and the Acropolis itself."

Where to Eat and What to Order

According to The Mediterran Travler, A must eat in Athen's is Meliartos Bakery! There is a ton of lunch spots to choose from, but this bakery is centrally located with a wide range of Greek cuisine. This is a popular coffee spot to try some classic greek pastries and order lunch. If you like coffee, then you have to try Greek coffee. Greek coffee is strong and brewed in a special small pot. The coffee is boiled for a long time to create a rich foamy layer on top of the coffee. For traditional Greek coffee, the grounds are left in the bottom of the cup when serving.
